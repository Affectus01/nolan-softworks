University Companion App

Objective:

Develop a student scheduler/student progress tracking application. Develop a mobile application for WGU students to track their terms, courses associated with each term, and assessment(s) associated with each course. The application will allow students to enter, edit, and delete term, course, and assessment data. It needs to provide summary and detailed views of courses for each term and provide alerts for upcoming performance and objective assessments. This application needs to use a SQLite database.
